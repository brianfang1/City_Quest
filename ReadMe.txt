City Quest

Visit brianfang1.github.io/City_Quest to play this game!

Gameplay:
This game requires 1 player. Use the arrow keys to move your character. 
Press the “spacebar” to use your briefcase to attack the enemies. 
By not attacking the pedestrians, more will spawn on the next stage, making it nearly impossible to move around.
The objective of the game is to get to the rightmost part of the screen to advance to the next stage.
Ultimately you must try to get to your office building(Rightmost part of the screen).
If you touch the pedestrians or cars, you will start from the leftmost side of the screen of each stage. 

Known Bugs:

Sometimes one or more Pedestrians will walk in a circle when they are stuck in a corner.This is not game breaking by any means because you can still attack them, but it is not intended for them to behave this way even if it only happens once a while. It is also possible for them to break out of the loop by themselves after a while.
